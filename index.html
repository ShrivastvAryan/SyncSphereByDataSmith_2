FAQs

Is Sync Sphere free to use?

Yes, Sync Sphere is completely free to use. You can create a profile, join groups, and connect with others without any cost.

Is my information secure on Sync Sphere?

We take your privacy seriously. Your information is protected, and you control what you share. We do not sell your data to third parties.

How do I customize my profile?

You can easily customize your profile by adding a photo, writing a short bio, and selecting your key interests. This help others understand more about you and find common ground.
